Lentil Dahl Instant Pot

This is a lentil dahl recipe adapted from a recipe found on  A Virtual Vegan

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 400g red lentils
  • 1 tin (400ml) light coconut milk
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 500g spinach
  • 750ml water

Spice Mix

  • thumb size piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Saute the onion in a few tablespoons of water for about 5 minutes until soft
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for a few more minutes
  3. Turn off the instant pot and add all the spices, salt and pepper. Leave to sit for one minute
  4. Add the water to the pot, mix well and scraping any stuck bits off the bottom of the pan
  5. Add the lentils, tomatoes and coconut milk. Do not stir. Cook on MANUAL for 8 minutes
  6. Allow the pressure to release naturally. Remove lid, give it a good stir and add the spinach. Allow spinach to wilt for a few minutes before serving
