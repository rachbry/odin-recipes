Moroccan Sweet Potato and Lentil Stew

This recipe used to be featured on the Instant Pot recipe page.

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 large or 2 small sweet potatoes
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 200g (1 cup) green lentils
  • 100g (1/2 cup) red lentils
  • 1 tin (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 50g raisins
  • 50g cashews
  • 500ml vegetable stock
  • 500g spinach or kale (stalks removed)   optional

Moroccan Spice Blend

  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • pinch of chilli flakes

Method

  1. Saute the onion in a few tablespoons of water for about 5 minutes until soft
  2. Add the garlic and cook for a few more minutes
  3. Add 1/2 of the spice mix, sweet potato, carrot, celery, raisins and cashews. Mix well and cook for another minute
  4. Add the stock and lentils, mixing well
  5. Cook on MANUAL for 10 minutes
  6. Allow the pressure to release naturally. Remove lid, give it a good stir and add the other half of the spice mix, tinned tomatoes and the spinach (if using). Leave on keep warm for a few minutes. Stir and serve.
